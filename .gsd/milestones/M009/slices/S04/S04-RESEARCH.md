# M009/S04: Performance Baseline & Bundle Comparison\n\n## Bundle Size Comparison\n\n| Metric | Before (S01 baseline) | After (S02+S03) | Delta |\n|--------|----------------------|-----------------|-------|\n| Total JS | 8,952 KB | 9,260 KB | +308 KB (+3.4%) |\n| Chunk count | 147 | 168 | +21 chunks |\n| Top chunk | three.js 1,368 KB x2 | three.js 1,368 KB x2 | unchanged |\n\nTotal size increased slightly due to dynamic import wrapper overhead — expected when splitting code into more granular chunks. The key win is **per-page initial load reduction**: tab overview pages no longer pull 4-8 D3 chart libraries in their initial bundle.\n\n## What was optimized\n\n1. **jsPDF dynamic import** — jsPDF (29MB node_modules), jspdf-autotable, and html-to-image are now loaded only when user clicks Export PDF. Zero bytes in initial bundle for any page.\n\n2. **Tab chart lazy-loading** — 18 chart component imports across 5 tab overview pages converted from static to next/dynamic with skeleton placeholders.\n\n3. **Individual chart pages** — left unchanged. Single-chart pages where the chart IS the content would just add latency without benefit.\n\n## Lighthouse Baseline (localhost, dev mode)\n\n| Page | Performance | FCP | LCP | TBT | CLS | SI |\n|------|-------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|----|\n| / (home) | 75 | 1.4s | 7.6s | 70ms | 0 | 1.5s |\n| /about | 77 | 0.8s | 6.4s | 100ms | 0 | 1.1s |\n\n**Note:** Dashboard pages require authentication and returned 404 to Lighthouse. Scores are dev-mode baselines (production builds on Vercel will score higher due to optimized serving, CDN, and SSR).\n\n## Remaining optimization opportunities\n\n- **three.js duplication** — Two identical 1.3MB chunks. Turbopack splitting artifact, not actionable from our code.\n- **Zod** — 912KB. Used by NextAuth and form validation. Could investigate lighter alternatives but high effort/low reward.\n- **LCP optimization** — 7.6s on homepage suggests large hero image or render-blocking resource. Worth investigating in a future milestone.\n- **Production Lighthouse** — Run against deployed Vercel URL for real-world scores."
