# S02: AI Narrative Engine for Report Generator

**Goal:** Replace template-based narratives in the report generator with LLM-generated analysis. The AI acts as an expert analyst interpreting the data, producing sections like Insights, Surprises, Concerns, Conclusions, Considerations, Opportunities.
**Demo:** After this: Report generator produces AI-generated sections: Impressions, Conclusions, Beware, Overlooked Opportunities for selected companies.
